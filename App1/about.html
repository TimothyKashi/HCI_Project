About

Team 13 aims to develop a web application that will recommend video games to users that want some recommendations on what games to play. The video game recommendations will be determined by administering a questionnaire to the users or offering an option to quickly choose a completely random game. The questionnaire will contain questions about previously played video games, personal tastes in genres, specific consols/marketplaces. The application will output different recommendations based on the answers, and will be able to suggest other games if the user is not satisfied with their recommendation.
